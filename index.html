Precious Metals (బంగారం & వెండి) *
Sona chandi chahe pehne huye ho, investment ho, ya amanat me ho — sab include karein.
Other Assets (ఇతర ఆస్తులు) *
Cash, bank balance, udhar diya paisa, business maal, shares, advance — sab include karein.
Liabilities (బాకీలు) *
Loan, EMI, maher, rent, salary, tax, bills — sirf current due amount minus karein.
